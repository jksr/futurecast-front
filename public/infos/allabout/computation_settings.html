Internal computational parameters

As the computation of the CASTp server is based on theorectical and algorithmic results of Computational Geometry, no parameters are needed a priori, except the Van der Waals radii of atoms or atomic groups. Here, we list the radii used by CASTp server.

Following the nomenclature in Ref[ddd], atoms are labeled in the form of "XnHm”, where X indicates the chemical symbol; n, the number of bonds, which, in most cases, is equivalent to say sp, sp2, or sp3 orbitals; Hm, the number (m) of hydrogen (H) atoms attached to the atom X. In this form, effects of hydrogen atoms are accounted implicitly in the atomic groups they belong to; otherwise, hydrogen atoms are ignored in computation.
