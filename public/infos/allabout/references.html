How to cite CASTp?

Your citation is really imortant for us. Please cite the following paper if you publish or present results using CASTp analysis:

  • Wei Tian, Chang Chen, Xue Lei, Jieling Zhao, Jie Liang. CASTp 3.0: computed atlas of surface topography of proteins . Nucleic Acids Res. 46(W1): W363–W367, 2018. PMID: 29860391 DOI: 10.1093/nar/gky473.
